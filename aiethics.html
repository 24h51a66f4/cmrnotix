Ethical Usage Guidelines for AI Tools

Follow these guidelines to ensure you are using AI tools ethically and responsibly in your studies:

  1. Understand AI Limitations: Recognize that AI tools are not perfect and can have limitations, biases, and make mistakes. Do not solely rely on AI without critical thinking.
  2. Verify AI Output: Always double-check and verify the information, code, or text generated by AI tools, especially for academic work.
  3. Respect Academic Integrity: Use AI tools as aids, not as substitutes for your own learning and original work. Understand your institution's policies on AI tool usage and plagiarism.
  4. Be Mindful of Bias: Be aware that AI models can reflect biases present in their training data. Critically evaluate AI outputs for potential biases, especially regarding sensitive topics like gender, race, or culture.
  5. Protect Your Privacy: Be cautious about the personal information you input into AI tools. Understand the privacy policies of the AI services you use.
  6. Use AI for Good: Employ AI tools to enhance your learning, creativity, and productivity ethically and responsibly. Avoid using AI for malicious purposes or academic dishonesty.
  7. Stay Informed: Keep learning about AI ethics and the evolving best practices for responsible AI usage.
